# SentientWeb — pitch & SEO reference

Editorial and investor-facing narrative. **SEO fields below must stay aligned with** `src/config/site.ts` when you change positioning or copy.

## One-liner

**SentientWeb** is the autonomous website agent: platform-agnostic AI that qualifies leads, books demos, answers questions from your knowledge base, and takes action — 24/7.

## Problem

B2B sites lose high-intent visitors to static forms, slow SDR follow-up, and generic chatbots that cannot qualify or book real meetings.

## Solution

A single engine with adapters for your stack: conversational lead qualification (BANT-lite), native demo booking, hybrid RAG over docs for accurate product Q&A, proactive engagement on high-intent pages, and CRM sync — with a roadmap toward content flywheel (e.g. doc-driven video, distribution).

## Phase 1 (live narrative)

- Inbound lead qualification tuned for SDR workflows  
- Demo booking with calendar integration  
- Sub-second time-to-first-token feel for “sentient” UX
